Why SmartHealth?

Healthcare data breaches hit all-time high in 2021, impacting 45M people and continues to grow as health systems and hospitals are under unprecedented stress from the pandemic.

How Does SmartHealth Help Humanity?

Leverages Blockchain technology to give the control back to you Secure login, creation and management of your own Electronic Health Record Need-to-know information provided by you to an authorized party

What is SmartHealth?

SmartHealth provides a secure platform using blockchain technology for exchanging Electronic Health Records authorized by the patient to specific helathcare providers.
